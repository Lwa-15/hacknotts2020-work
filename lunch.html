Sandwich

Ingredients: bread, butter and sandwich fillings.
Equipment: a plate and a knife.
Instructions:
1) put bread on the plate
2) slather butter on the bread using the knife
3) put fillings on one slice of buttered bread
4) put other slice of bread on top
5) cut in half with knife
6) et voila

Tomato Soup

Ingredients: tomato soup.
Equipment: bowl, microwave and spoon.
Instructions:
1) pour the soup into the bowl
2) put the bowl in the microwave for two minutes
