Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients: spaghetti, bolognese sauce, mince (may be vegetarian/vegan/meat) and cheese.
Equipment: 2 saucepans, a colander and 2 wooden spoons.
Instructions:
1) heat up two saucepans on the hob
2) add mince to one saucepan and let it sizzle for 5 minutes
3) add sauce to the same pan and stir until it smells nice (around 15 minutes)
4) add spaghetti to the second pan, stir and heat until it is floppy and soft (around 20mins)
5) once the spaghetti is cooked, drain the water with a colander
6) add the spaghetti, then cooked mince to a plate and season with cheese (if needed)
7) et voila

Pasta

Ingredients: penne pasta, pasta sauce and cheese.
Equipment: saucepan, colander and wooden spoon.
Instructions:
1) heat up a saucepan
2) add penne pasta and sauce
3) stir for 20 minutes
4) drain the water with a colander
5) put pasta in a bowl and season with cheese (if needed)
6) et voila

Cheesy Nachos

Ingredients: nachos, salsa and cheese.
Equipment: a baking tray.
Instructions:
1) put the nachos on the baking tray and add cheese
2) put the baking tray in the oven for 10 minutes
3) remove the tray and serve with a side of salsa (if needed)
4) et voila
